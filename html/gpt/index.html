ABOUT OUR SERVER

PREMIUM ROLEPLAY EXPERIENCE
NCHub was founded in 2022 with the goal of providing an immersive roleplay experience through custom scripts, exclusive features, and a dedicated community. In addition to its innovative developments, NCHub offers premium script packages like V2, allowing server owners to enhance and customize their gameplay.
Roleplay at all times - Stay in character and respect the RP experience
No RDM (Random Deathmatch) - Don't kill without valid RP reason
No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) - Don't use vehicles as weapons
Be respectful to all players - No harassment or discrimination
Follow staff instructions - Staff decisions are final
Report bugs - Don't exploit them for personal gain
